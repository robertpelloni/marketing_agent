# Product Hunt Launch Checklist

- [ ] Logo (256x256 PNG)

Give your AI assistants persistent memory and access to 26,000+ tools.

## Launch Day

### Timing

- **Best time**: Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PST
- **Why**: Fresh start, full day of visibility

### Steps

1. Submit at 12:01 AM PST
2. Post on Twitter with #ProductHunt
3. Post on Reddit (r/SideProject, r/InternetIsBeautiful)
4. Post on Hacker News
5. Email supporters to upvote
6. Respond to every comment

### Upvote Strategy

- Ask friends/family to upvote
- Post in maker communities
- Share on social media
- Engage with comments

## Post-Launch

### Follow-up

- Thank everyone who upvoted
- Respond to all feedback
- Share results on Twitter
- Write a blog post about the experience

### Metrics to Track

- Upvotes
- Comments
- Website traffic
- GitHub stars
- Sign-ups
